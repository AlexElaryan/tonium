Legend of the Tonium's Universe

At the dawn of time, when the universe was just beginning to exist, there was one primordial element — Thonium. This element was the embodiment of pure energy and matter, maintaining the harmony and stability of all things. Its power and energy permeated every corner of the cosmos, shaping the laws of physics and creating the basis for life. But one day something incredible happened — a cataclysm that changed the course of history forever.

Great Quantum Decay

Thonium, possessing infinite energy, entered into an unstable interaction with the environment. As a result of this interaction, subatomic particles began to oscillate, leading to a cascading quantum explosion. In a flash of unprecedented power, Thonium disintegrated into 118 separate elements. These elements scattered throughout the universe, creating new planets and forming the basis for diverse life forms.

Planets and Their Resources

Each of the 118 planets in the universe contains one of the elements that can be collected and used. These elements became the building blocks for civilizations, technologies and cultures.

Tonium Quest

However, the greatest adventure is just beginning. Recreating the original element Thonium is possible, but to do this it is necessary to collect seven special elements. Every day, Toniy changes his internal code, and to recreate it, seven new elements must be found.

Real Reward

Thonium has not only historical and scientific value, but also real value. Once you collect it, you can exchange Thonium on the market for real currency — the TON cryptocurrency, at the rate of 1 Thonium to 1 TON. This opens up endless possibilities for players, turning virtual achievements into real value.
